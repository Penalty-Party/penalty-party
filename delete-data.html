Data Deletion Request

Penalty Party · JJ Solutions · Last updated: 24 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of the data associated with your use of the Penalty Party mobile game, provided by JJ Solutions.

Penalty Party does not use registered accounts. Your data is stored against an anonymous, device-generated player ID, and we do not hold your name or email address. You can delete your data directly inside the app at any time.

Delete your data in the app

  1. Open Penalty Party.
  2. Go to Settings → Legal.
  3. Tap Delete Data and confirm.
This permanently removes the data associated with your player ID, including your progress and match history. This action cannot be undone.

Alternative: request by email

If you have already uninstalled the app or cannot reach the in-app option, email penaltypartythegame@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my data" and include your in-game player code (shown under Multiplayer). We will delete the associated data within 30 days and reply to confirm.

What is deleted

  • Your anonymous player profile and identifier.
  • Your game progress (cosmetics, tickets, achievements, settings).
  • Your online match history and any active matches.
  • Your notification token.

What may be kept

  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics and crash-diagnostic data that is no longer linked to your player ID, retained for up to 14 months to help improve the app.
  • Any records we are required to retain by law.

For more on how we handle data, see our Privacy Policy.
