Privacy Policy

Penalty Party · Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Penalty Party mobile game ("the app", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. The app is provided by JJ Solutions, who is the data controller responsible for your data. You can contact us at penaltypartythegame@gmail.com.

Penalty Party does not require you to create an account, and does not collect your name, email address, or phone number.

Data we collect

  • Anonymous player ID. When you first open the app, a unique, anonymous identifier is generated to save your progress and run online matches. It is not linked to your real-world identity.
  • Game progress. Your cosmetics, tickets, achievements and settings.
  • Match data. The picks, scores and results of your online matches, so multiplayer can function.
  • Notification token. A device token used to send you "your move" and match notifications.
  • Usage and diagnostics. App interaction events, approximate location (derived from your IP address, at a city/country level), device identifiers, and crash logs & performance diagnostics, collected through Google Analytics for Firebase and Firebase Crashlytics.

How we use it

  • To run the game and save your progress.
  • To operate online multiplayer (matchmaking, gameplay, notifications).
  • To understand how the app is used and to fix crashes and bugs.

Who has access

Your data is processed on our behalf by Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging, Analytics and Crashlytics), acting as our service provider. We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties for their own purposes. Google may process data in the United States and other countries; Google maintains data-transfer safeguards including certification under the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework.

Security

All data collected by the app is encrypted in transit using TLS.

Data retention

Your player profile and progress are kept while the app is in use. Anonymised analytics and diagnostic data are retained for up to 14 months. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

Your rights

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the right under the GDPR to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your data, and to data portability. Because the app uses an anonymous identifier, you can exercise these rights by contacting us with your in-game player code (see the deletion instructions below). Our lawful basis for processing is our legitimate interest in operating and improving the game. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (in the Netherlands, the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

Deleting your data

You can delete your data in the app at any time under Settings → Legal → Delete Data. If you can no longer access the app, email penaltypartythegame@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my data" and your in-game player code. Full details: Data Deletion page.

Children

Penalty Party is intended for users aged 13 and over and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if future versions add new features). Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
